Professional Esports

A summary of Raj "Snax" Varma's competitive esports records across PUBG Mobile and BGMI (compiled from Liquipedia).

Dec 2019 - Nov 2021

TeamIND

A legacy era where Snax earned national acclaim. Acted as a front-line assaulter representing India in PMWL 2020: East and multiple PMPL regional seasons.

Nov 2021 - Jan 2022

Velocity Gaming

Joined the powerhouse Velocity Gaming roster during the initial transition into Battlegrounds Mobile India (BGMI). Secured a historic 3rd place finish at BGIS 2021.

Feb 2022 - Aug 2022

His final professional roster run. Snax and XO dominated the mid-2022 circuit, taking consecutive gold podiums at Skyesports Grand Slam and Upthrust India Rising.
