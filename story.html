Biography

THE STORY OF SNAX

Born and raised in Hyderabad, Raj "Snax" Varma’s journey is a cinematic testament to resilience, tactical genius, and deep family devotion. Before becoming one of India's leading digital creators, Raj grew up in a humble middle-class household, watching his parents make daily compromises to build a future for their family.

From local cyber cafes to representing the country on international stages, his esports name "Snax" has become synonymous with elite mechanical execution. As a retired pro and key content force for S8UL Esports, he continues to lead the community by showing that the greatest victory is lifting those who supported you from the start.

Interactive Journey Timeline

1999 - 2016

Early Life

Grew up in Hyderabad, India. His childhood was shaped by a strong family support structure and a deep desire to someday ease the financial hardships of his parents.

"I saw my parents skip their own needs to buy us books. I promised myself I would change our story."
2017

Gaming Discovery

Discovered competitive gaming in local internet cafes. Excelled in shooter games, demonstrating extreme hand-eye coordination and spatial awareness.

2018 - 2019

PUBG Mobile Beginnings

Began playing PUBG Mobile competitively with local squads like Learn From Past. Formulated his signature DP-28 long-range sprays, earning the community title "DP God".

"People said mobile gaming wasn't a career. I just kept my head down and perfected the DP-28 recoil."
2020 - 2021

Rise In Esports (TeamIND)

Signed with TeamIND, one of India's legacy esports squads. Competed globally at the PUBG Mobile World League 2020: East, showcasing tactical leadership.

2021 - 2022

Content Creation Era

Expanded into full-time entertainment. Uploaded guides, funny challenges, and lifestyle blogs, crossing 1 million subscribers in a record timeline.

"Streaming is about connection. People came for the gameplay, they stayed for the conversations."
2022

Building A Community (Team XO)

Joined Team XO, winning consecutive major tournaments like Skyesports Grand Slam and Upthrust India Rising. Solidified XO as India's top BGMI lineup.

Present Day

S8UL & The Mumbai Dream

Retired from active roster play to join S8UL Esports as a content creator in Mumbai. Fulfilled his dream of buying a premium Mumbai home for his parents.

"Gifting that house key to my mom and dad... that is my real MVP trophy. Everything else is secondary."
