FAQ Section

How can I collaborate with SNAX?

For custom streaming lobbies, YouTube collab videos, or gaming crossovers, reach out using the "Brand Collaboration" option in the form above or email our team directly at business@snaxgaming.com.

How can brands partner with SNAX?

We support end-to-end activations, high-impact YouTube integrations, social endorsement posts, custom vlogs, and gaming gear review placements. Let's design a custom campaign together. Choose "Sponsorship Proposal" to begin.

How can I invite SNAX to an event?

For gaming events, college panels, tech shows, or creators gatherings, please select "Event Invitation" in the form dropdown. Provide location, budget overview, target audience, and dates. Our S8UL management handles the scheduling.

How quickly can I expect a response?

Business proposals, sponsorships, and event requests are reviewed by talent management daily. You can expect a response within 24 to 48 business hours. General community messages are appreciated and reviewed as schedule permits.
