Hailing from Hyderabad, Raj "Snax" Varma is one of India's most prominent ex-esports athletes and content creator for S8UL. Best known for the legendary "DP Burst God" for his signature DP-28 tactical controls, Snax connects with millions of fans through pro gameplay, tournament watchparties, and lifestyle vlogging.

Whether he's collaborating with global brands or hosting high-energy gaming and watchparty streams,From tactical BGMI analysis to behind-the-scene vlogs, there's always something fresh for his 2.3 M+ subscribers.
